2. 로지스틱회귀분석¶

  • 단순선형회귀 : logit($p$) = log($p$/$1-p$) = $\beta_0$ + $\beta_1$$X$

  • 다중선형회귀 : logit($p$) = log($p$/$1-p$) = $\beta_0$ + $\beta_2$$X_1$ + ... + $\beta_p$$X_p$
